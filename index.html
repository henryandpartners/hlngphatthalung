HLNG Art Residency

Located at 200 years old shophouse in Phatthalung City. HLNG Art Residency launch their first open for international creator to reside, work and explore Phatthalung city at their own pace.


Phatthalung, Thailand

a charming province in southern Thailand, offers a unique blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and serene landscapes that make it a captivating destination. Known for the stunning Thale Noi Lake, one of Thailand's most important wetlands, visitors can witness thousands of migrating water birds, particularly the iconic pink lotus blooms that carpet the waters at dawn. Beyond the lake, the province boasts the dramatic Khao Ok Thalu mountain, often called the "Phatthalung mountain," which offers panoramic views and a challenging climb. For those interested in local culture, the lively markets and traditional villages provide an authentic glimpse into Southern Thai life, while the unique "Manora" folk dance performances are a cultural highlight. Phatthalung's tranquil atmosphere, combined with its rich biodiversity and cultural depth, provides a refreshing escape from the bustling tourist trails.

Residency Information

Goal

Our mission is to provide a supportive and inspiring environment for artists to push the boundaries of their practice, foster cross-cultural dialogue, and connect with the vibrant Phatthalung community and connect with Thai art scene."

Open Call

What we provide

1. Free 3 months accommodation and working space (100sqm. Bedroom and Studio )
2. Share toilets, kitchen, tools and common area.
3. Invitation Letter
4. Orientation and visit to our network and friends around Phatthalung City

Expectation towards artists 

1. Artist must cover their own Flight, Visa, Insurance and Expenses
2.The skill to explore city by themselves and survival instinct to avoid risk and danger
3.Public talk, a workshop for the local community, or an open studio day.
4.An option to donate an artwork create during residency for HLNG Art Residency archive.
